## About the Collection

This collection began when I was young with an odd assortment of turtle figurines. As the years passed I became more intentional about adding items to my collection, and other people also began to contribute to it. On this site you can find information about: 

- what type of turtle it is
- when it joined the collection
- who purchased the turtle
- where it came from
- what materials and techniques were used to create the turtle
- the size of the figure
